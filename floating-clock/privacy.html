Floating Clock Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Floating Clock is a macOS utility that displays a simple floating clock on your desktop.

Floating Clock is designed to be lightweight and private. It does not collect, upload, or share personal data.

Information We Collect

Floating Clock does not collect personal information.

We do not collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your location
  • Your files
  • Your usage analytics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any personal content from your Mac

Local Settings

Floating Clock may save app settings locally on your Mac, such as:

  • Clock position
  • Font choice
  • Font size
  • Color choice
  • Display preferences
  • Launch at login preference, if enabled

These settings stay on your device.

Network Usage

Floating Clock does not require a network connection for its core functionality. The app does not upload data to any server.

Purchases

If Floating Clock includes Apple App Store purchases or paid download functionality, Apple may process purchase-related information according to Apple’s own policies.

Floating Clock does not receive your payment card details.

Third-Party Services

Floating Clock does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

Children’s Privacy

Floating Clock is not designed to collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app changes in the future. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
