Floating Clock Support

Floating Clock is a minimal macOS utility that displays a floating transparent digital clock on your desktop.

What can Floating Clock do?

Floating Clock lets you:

  • Show a floating digital clock on your desktop
  • Keep the clock above other windows
  • Use a transparent, minimal display
  • Choose font, size, and color options
  • Position the clock where you want it
  • Use the clock without interrupting normal Mac operations

Common Questions

Can I move the clock?

Yes. You can drag the clock to your preferred position if the app version supports repositioning.

Can I change the font or size?

Yes. Floating Clock includes simple customization options such as font, size, and color.

Does the clock interfere with clicking other apps?

Floating Clock is designed to stay visible while remaining lightweight and non-disruptive.

Does Floating Clock collect my data?

No. Floating Clock does not collect personal data or upload your information.

Does Floating Clock require internet?

No. Floating Clock works locally on your Mac.

Troubleshooting

The clock is hard to see

Try changing the font color, size, or background/opacity settings if available.

The clock is not visible

Try:

  • Reopening the app
  • Checking whether the clock is positioned off-screen
  • Restarting the app
  • Checking display settings if you use multiple monitors

Launch at login is not working

Check macOS System Settings:

System Settings → General → Login Items

Make sure Floating Clock is allowed to open at login.
